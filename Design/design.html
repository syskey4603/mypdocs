Tips

  • Design Cycle: Show all stages: Inquiring, Developing Ideas, Creating Solution, Evaluating.
  • Use Real Evidence: Screenshots, sketches, feedback, test results.
  • Justify Tools: Explain why you chose specific software, materials, or techniques.
  • Highlight Iteration: Reflect improvements through drafts, versions, or retests.
  • Link ATL skills: Mention how you planned, collaborated, or solved problems creatively.
