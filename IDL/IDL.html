Tips

  • Know the Paper: It's a 36-mark paper: 12 marks each for Criteria A, B, and C.
  • Timing: The official IDL paper is sent by the IB in April.
  • Criterion A: Evaluate the reliability and relevance of sources across disciplines.
  • Criterion B: Synthesize perspectives from at least two disciplines clearly and logically.
  • Criterion C: Reflect on the implications and express clear judgment, often including a personal viewpoint.
  • Use PRM: Refer to the IDPRM (Interdisciplinary Performance Requirement Materials) for question types and structure.

Interdisciplinary Learning (IDL)

IDL tasks assess your ability to evaluate, synthesize, and reflect using at least two disciplines.
