Tips

  • Understand Concepts: Focus on causality, sustainability, and spatial interactions.
  • Case Studies: Use real-world examples with specific dates, figures, and evaluation.
  • Command Terms: Always break down what is being asked: describe, evaluate, compare, etc.
  • Structure: Write paragraphs with clear points, supporting evidence, and mini conclusions.
  • Maps & Data: Use GIS, diagrams, and maps effectively when provided.
